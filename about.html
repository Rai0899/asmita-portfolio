About

I write content that earns its place on the page.

I'm a content writer, editor, and strategist based in Kolkata, India. My work sits at the intersection of editorial rigour and digital strategy — I care about what gets written and why, not just whether it goes out on time.

Over the past two years I've worked across EdTech, government heritage, finance, robotics, and digital marketing — writing and editing for platforms that needed content that actually performs: in search, with readers, and against a brief.

At IEMA Research & Development, I ran editorial operations for a team of five, managing a pipeline of 200+ pieces. I built the quality framework from scratch, introduced audience-first commissioning, and helped grow readership by 45% year-on-year — without expanding the team.

Before that, I worked as a content writer at Collegedunia, as an editor at MyMegaMinds, and as a communications intern at Smile Foundation. My MA in English Literature from Symbiosis College of Arts & Commerce, Pune taught me to read slowly enough to understand why a sentence works — or doesn't.

What I Actually Do

I write long-form editorial, SEO and GEO-optimised content, product explainers, finance and investment analysis, STEM education guides, e-commerce and category copy, and case studies. I also edit — the kind of editing that works on the person writing as much as the piece they submit.

I've worked on national heritage content (the National Postal Museum Delhi interactive kiosk), robotics education platforms, finance publications, and SaaS product blogs. The range is deliberate: I'm most useful to teams who need someone who can shift register, understand search intent, and produce content that serves a real reader with a real question.
