Product
Relief Sun, Rice + Probiotics SPF50+ PA++++ (50ml)

This is a sunscreen you will actually want to wear every day. Light, vegan, and made with 30% rice extract alongside a grain probiotics complex that protects skin while calming it — no white cast, no greasy feel left behind once it sets in.

With SPF50+ PA++++, it gives strong protection against both UVA and UVB rays. Niacinamide helps even out skin tone and keep your complexion looking clear. The texture feels closer to a light moisturiser than a typical sunscreen, so it goes on smoothly under makeup without pilling or clumping.

Best for everyday use across most skin types, including sensitive skin, since the formula is gentle and made with comfort in mind.

How to Use

Apply a generous amount as the last step in your skincare routine, about 30 minutes before going out in the sun. Reapply every two to three hours to keep your skin protected through the day.
