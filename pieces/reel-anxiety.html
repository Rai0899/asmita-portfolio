Held It Together for Years

Category
Scripts · Personal Branding
Format
Reel script, direct-to-camera (28 seconds)
Audience
Followers of a personal brand built on mental health honesty
Tone
Flat, unhurried, deliberately undramatic

Editor's note: Written for a personal brand reel where the entire effect depends on restraint. No music, no cuts, no performance cues that soften the line being delivered.

[HOOK — 0:00 to 0:03]

(No setup. No music. Asmita looks at the camera like she is finally saying the thing.)

You are fine, though. You are always fine. That is what you tell everyone. That is what you tell yourself.

[BODY — 0:04 to 0:20]

(Still. Dry. Like she has told this story once before and is done softening it.)

The thing about anxiety nobody tells you is that the people who have it the worst are usually the ones you would never guess.

Showing up. Hitting deadlines. Laughing at the right moments.

I was so good at looking okay that even I believed it most days.

Until I was sitting at my desk, completely ordinary Tuesday, and something just gave way. Hands shaking. Chest tight. Inbox full.

I excused myself, sat in a bathroom stall for eleven minutes, pulled myself together, and went back in.

Nobody noticed. That was the most terrifying part.

Everyone thinks you are sorted. Calm. Handling it.

And maybe that is the loneliest part. Being the most chaotic internally and the most composed externally. Every single day.

[CLOSE — 0:21 to 0:28]

(Flat delivery. Eye contact.)

"If you just read that and felt seen, Unfiltered by Asmita is for you.

Not for people who have it figured out. For people who are exhausted from acting as they do.

Comment 'mask' if this is you. I actually want to know.
