Category
E-commerce · Beauty & Fashion
Format
Category page copy

Intro (shown above the product grid)

Serums are where your skincare routine gets specific. Whether you are chasing brightness, hydration, smoother texture, or fewer breakouts, a good serum delivers concentrated actives exactly where your skin needs them. Explore by concern below to find the one that fits your routine.

Filter Blurbs

  • Vitamin C — for brightening and fading dark spots
  • Hyaluronic Acid — for deep, lasting hydration
  • Niacinamide — for pores, redness, and oil control
  • Retinol — for fine lines and skin renewal

Why This Structure Works

Category pages do two jobs at once: help someone who already knows what they want filter quickly, and gently inform someone just browsing what each ingredient actually does — without turning the page into a skincare lecture.
