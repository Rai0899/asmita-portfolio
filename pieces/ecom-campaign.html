08c · Seasonal Campaign — Festive Glow Edit

Category
E-commerce · Beauty & Fashion
Format
Seasonal campaign copy
Campaign
Diwali Sale

Banner

Headline: Your Festive Glow Starts Here
Sub-line: Up to 40% off skincare and makeup must-haves, because this season you deserve to shine as much as the diyas.

Campaign Page Intro

Festive season means more parties, more photos, and no time for a ten-step routine. The Festive Glow Edit brings together the skincare and makeup picks that actually deliver on camera and in person, including brightening serums, long-wear bases, and bold lip colours, all at festive prices for a limited time.

Social Caption (paired with a product carousel)

Lights, camera, glow. Our Festive Glow Edit is live, with up to 40% off the skincare and makeup edit you will actually reach for this season. Link in bio before it's gone.

Why This Works

Seasonal copy has a tighter job than regular product copy: create urgency through a limited-time discount while still feeling like a gift rather than a panic sale. The headline leads with how the customer wants to feel; the discount comes second.
